Lasagna

Description

This lasagna recipe is something special.
I once brought to a BBQ picnic and was complimented several times.

Ingredients

  • 3/4 lb beef
  • 1 lb mild sausage
  • 16 oz Ricotta Cheese
  • 16 oz Whole Milk Mozzerella
  • 2ea 6oz cans of tomatoe paste
  • 4 cloves of garlic
  • 1 onion chopped
  • 1ea 28oz can of crushed tomatoes
  • 1 lb package of lasagna noodles
  • 2 tsp Italian Seasoning
  • 1 tsp Fennel
  • 1 tsp Salt
  • 1 tsp Sugar
  • 2 tsp chopped parsley
  • 2 eggs

Directions

In Dutch oven brown the sausage, beef and onion. Add tomatoe paste, crushed tomatoes, salt, garlic, fennel, Italian seasoning. Simmer covered on low for 1-1/2 hours. In a bowl mix the ricotta cheese with the parsley and 2 eggs. Put in the refrigerator. With the filling meat sauce done get a 11 x 17 glass lasagna tray and scoop 1/3 of the sauce along the bottom of dish. Layer with lasagna noodles (1 layer) and spread 1/2 of the ricotta cheese on the noodles. Add some slices of Mozzerella then another 1/3 of the meat sauce. Add a layer of noodles and spread the remaining Ricotta cheese then more Mozzerella. Bake covered with foil in a preheated oven for 25 minutes. Remove foil and bake for another 25 minutes.
