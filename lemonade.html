Lemonade

Description

At a picnic in the summer or for a tart citrus this simple recipe will win over your taste buds.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of fresh sqeezed lemon juice
  • 3/4 cup of white granulated sugar
  • 54 oz of filtered water

Directions

  • Mix the lemon juice and sugar in a jar. Cap the jar and shake vigoursly for 30 seconds.
    • Let the mixture sit for 15 minutes on the counter. Empty contents in a 64 oz or larger pitcher. Add 54 oz of water and stir.
