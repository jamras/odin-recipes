Grilled Hamburgers

Description

Grilled hamburgers are a classic American dish. The meat is grilled over an open flame, then served with a variety of toppings.

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs of 85 percent lean hamburger
  • 1/2 cup of bread crumbs
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/4 cup of milk
  • 1 cup of chopped onions (optional)

Directions

Mix all ingredients in a large bowl. Form patties by hand to desired size. Grill on a hot grill until 3/4 of the side of the patty is light brown. Flip burgers and grill for anoter 5 minutes. It is important to only flip the burgers only once.
